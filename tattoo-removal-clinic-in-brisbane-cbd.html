Frequently Asked Questions About Tattoo Removal Clinic

How much does tattoo removal cost in Brisbane?
At Think Again Laser Clinic Brisbane, tattoo removal costs typically range from $100-$450 per session, depending on your tattoo's size, color, and complexity. Small tattoos (up to 5cm) start at $100, medium tattoos (5-10cm) from $200, and larger pieces from $300. We offer package deals with savings of up to 20% when booking multiple sessions. During your free consultation in our CBD clinic, we'll assess your tattoo and provide an exact quote based on your specific case.
How many sessions will I need to remove my tattoo completely?
Most clients require 6-12 sessions for complete removal, with treatments spaced 6-8 weeks apart. The exact number depends on factors like ink colors (black and red typically remove faster than blues and greens), tattoo age, skin type, and location on the body. During your initial consultation at our Brisbane CBD clinic, we'll examine your tattoo and provide a more accurate estimate of sessions needed. We use the latest PicoSure technology, which often requires fewer sessions than traditional Q-switched lasers.
Is the tattoo removal process painful and what aftercare is required?
The sensation is often described as similar to getting a tattoo - like a rubber band snap against your skin. We use medical-grade numbing cream to minimize discomfort. After treatment, you might experience redness and slight swelling for 2-3 days. Brisbane's warm climate means extra sun protection is crucial - we recommend keeping the area covered and using SPF50+ sunscreen. We provide detailed aftercare instructions, including specific products suitable for Queensland's humidity and climate conditions.
