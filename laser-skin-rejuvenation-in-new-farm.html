How many sessions will I need to remove my tattoo completely?
Most tattoos require 6-12 sessions for complete removal, with treatments spaced 6-8 weeks apart. The exact number depends on factors like ink colors (black and red typically remove faster than blues and greens), ink depth, and your skin type. During your initial consultation, we'll examine your tattoo using our advanced laser assessment technology and provide a more precise estimate for your specific case. We've found that professional tattoos typically need 8-12 sessions, while amateur tattoos often require 4-8 sessions.
Is the laser removal process painful and what aftercare is required?
The sensation is often described as similar to a rubber band snap against your skin. We use the latest PicoSure laser technology and provide numbing cream 30 minutes before treatment to minimize discomfort. For aftercare, we recommend keeping the area clean and protected from sun exposure for at least 2 weeks. We provide all clients with a detailed aftercare pack including antiseptic cream and instructions. Most clients in Brisbane's climate can return to normal activities immediately, though swimming and excessive sweating should be avoided for 48 hours.
