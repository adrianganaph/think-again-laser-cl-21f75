Frequently Asked Questions About Sensitive Area Tattoo Removal

How much does tattoo removal cost at your Carindale clinic?
The cost varies depending on your tattoo's size, colors, and density. As a guide, small tattoos (size of a credit card) typically start from $150 per session, while larger pieces can range from $250-$500 per session. We offer package deals when you pre-purchase multiple sessions, saving up to 20%. During your free consultation at our Carindale clinic, we'll assess your tattoo and provide an exact quote based on your specific case.
How many sessions will I need for complete removal?
Most clients require 6-12 sessions for complete removal, with treatments spaced 6-8 weeks apart. Factors affecting the number of sessions include tattoo age, ink colors (black and red typically fade faster than blues and greens), skin tone, and tattoo location. During your initial consultation, we'll examine your tattoo using our advanced assessment technology to provide a more accurate estimate for your specific case.
Is tattoo removal in sensitive areas more painful than regular tattoo removal?
While sensitive area removal can be more uncomfortable, we use advanced cooling systems and medical-grade numbing creams to minimize discomfort. Our PicoSure laser technology also causes less trauma to the skin compared to traditional Q-switched lasers. We can adjust the treatment intensity and take breaks as needed during your session. Most clients describe the sensation as similar to having a rubber band snapped against the skin.

What aftercare is required following sensitive area tattoo removal at your Carindale clinic?
We provide detailed aftercare instructions and a complimentary aftercare kit with each treatment.
