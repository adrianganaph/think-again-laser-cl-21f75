Frequently Asked Questions About Tattoo Removal for Minors (with parental consent)

How much does tattoo removal cost for minors at your Toowong clinic?
Our tattoo removal prices for minors start from $99 per session for small tattoos (size of a credit card or smaller). Medium-sized tattoos range from $199-299 per session, while larger pieces are individually quoted. We require a parent or legal guardian to be present for the initial consultation and to provide written consent. We also offer special student discounts and payment plans to help make the treatment more affordable for younger clients.
How many sessions will I need to remove my tattoo completely?
Most tattoos require 6-12 sessions for complete removal, with sessions spaced 6-8 weeks apart. During your free consultation at our Toowong clinic, we'll assess your specific tattoo's characteristics (age, colors, depth, location) to provide a more accurate estimate. Black and grey tattoos typically require fewer sessions than colored ones. We use the latest PicoSure technology which often reduces the total number of sessions needed compared to older laser systems.
Is parental consent required for tattoo removal if I'm under 18?
Yes, we legally require written parental consent for all clients under 18 years old. A parent or legal guardian must attend the initial consultation and sign our consent forms. They must also be present for the first treatment session. For subsequent sessions, we require written permission from the parent/guardian, though they don't need to be present for every treatment unless they choose to be.
Does tattoo removal hurt and what's the recovery process like?
Most clients describe the sensation as similar to having a rubber band snapped against the skin. We use medical-grade numbing cream to minimize discomfort. After treatment, you might experience some redness, swelling, and mild blistering for 3-7 days. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and our Toowong clinic staff are available by phone if you have any concerns during recovery. You can return to school/work immediately, but should avoid swimming and direct sun exposure for 1-2 weeks.
What if my tattoo doesn't fully remove - do you offer any guarantees?
While we can't guarantee 100% removal on every tattoo due to variables like ink type and skin tone, we offer a satisfaction guarantee program. If you don't see significant fading after 3 sessions, we'll provide a complimentary session. We document your progress with photos at each visit and adjust our treatment approach as needed. Our PicoSure laser technology has a proven track record, particularly effective on the darker inks common in amateur tattoos that many younger clients have.
