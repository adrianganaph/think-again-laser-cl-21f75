Frequently Asked Questions About Tattoo Fading for Cover-Up

How much does tattoo fading for a cover-up typically cost in Brisbane?
At Think Again Laser Clinic Brisbane, fading sessions for cover-ups typically range from $150-$350 per session, depending on your tattoo's size. Most cover-up preparations require 2-4 sessions to achieve optimal fading for your new artwork. We offer free consultations where we can assess your specific tattoo and provide an exact quote. We also provide payment plans through ZipPay and Afterpay to help manage the cost.
How many sessions will I need before I can get my cover-up tattoo?
The number of fading sessions needed varies based on several factors: the density of your existing tattoo, its colours (black is typically easier to fade), and your skin type. Most clients need 2-4 sessions spaced 8-12 weeks apart to achieve the level of fading required for a successful cover-up. During your free consultation at our South Brisbane clinic, we'll assess your tattoo and work with your chosen cover-up artist to determine the optimal fading level needed.
Is the laser treatment painful and what's the recovery like in Brisbane's climate?
The treatment feels similar to a rubber band snap against your skin. We use the latest cooling technology and numbing options to minimize discomfort. Given Brisbane's warm climate, we provide specific aftercare instructions to protect your treated area from the sun and heat. Recovery typically involves some redness and swelling for 2-3 days. We recommend avoiding swimming pools and beaches for at least 7 days post-treatment, particularly relevant for our Queensland lifestyle.
