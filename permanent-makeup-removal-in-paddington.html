Frequently Asked Questions About Permanent Makeup Removal

How much does tattoo removal cost at your Paddington clinic?
The cost varies depending on the size, color, and complexity of your tattoo. At Think Again Laser Clinic Brisbane, small tattoos (up to 5cm) typically start from $150 per session, medium tattoos (5-10cm) from $250, and larger pieces are quoted individually. We offer package deals for multiple sessions, which most clients need. During your free consultation at our Paddington clinic, we'll assess your tattoo and provide an exact quote with recommended number of sessions.
How many sessions will I need for complete removal?
Most clients require 6-12 sessions for complete tattoo removal, with sessions spaced 6-8 weeks apart. The exact number depends on factors like ink color (black and red typically remove faster than blues and greens), ink depth, skin type, and tattoo age. During your initial consultation, we'll examine your tattoo using our advanced laser assessment technology and provide a more accurate estimate for your specific case. We find that professional tattoos typically need more sessions than amateur ones.
Is the tattoo removal process painful at Think Again Laser?
While the process does involve some discomfort, we use advanced cooling systems and numbing options to minimize pain. Most clients describe the sensation as similar to a rubber band snap. We provide complimentary medical-grade numbing cream 30 minutes before treatment, and our state-of-the-art Q-switched laser technology is designed to be as comfortable as possible while remaining effective. Our experienced technicians in Paddington are trained to adjust the treatment intensity based on your comfort level.
Are your laser technicians qualified and licensed in Brisbane?
Yes, all our technicians are fully qualified and licensed in Queensland. They hold current laser safety certifications and have completed advanced training in laser tattoo removal techniques. Our clinic meets all Brisbane City Council health regulations and we're fully insured. What aftercare is required following treatment at your clinic?
After each session, we provide detailed aftercare instructions and a complimentary aftercare kit. Key requirements include keeping the area clean and dry for 24-48 hours, applying our specially formulated healing cream, and avoiding sun exposure for at least 2 weeks. We recommend using SPF50+ sunscreen on the treated area (once healed) between sessions. You'll need to avoid swimming pools and intense exercise for 3-4 days post-treatment.
