Frequently Asked Questions About Aftercare Products for Tattoo Removal

How much does laser tattoo removal cost in Brisbane?
At Think Again Laser Clinic Chermside, tattoo removal costs typically range from $100-$350 per session, depending on the size of your tattoo. Small tattoos (up to 5cm) start at $100, medium tattoos (5-10cm) range from $150-$250, and large tattoos (10cm+) start from $250. We offer package deals for multiple sessions, which can save you up to 20%. During your free consultation, we'll assess your tattoo and provide an exact quote based on its size, colours, and density.
How many sessions will I need to remove my tattoo completely?
Most clients require 6-12 sessions for complete removal, with sessions spaced 6-8 weeks apart. However, this varies based on several factors specific to your tattoo: its age, colours used (black and red typically fade faster than blues and greens), depth of ink, and your skin type. During your initial consultation at our Chermside clinic, we'll examine your tattoo and provide a more accurate estimate of required sessions. We also take progress photos after each session to track the fading process.
Is laser tattoo removal painful and what's the recovery like?
The sensation is often described as similar to a rubber band snap against your skin. We use advanced cooling systems and can apply numbing cream if needed. Post-treatment, you might experience redness, slight swelling, and a sensation similar to a mild sunburn for 2-3 days. We provide comprehensive aftercare products and instructions specific to Brisbane's climate, including recommendations for sun protection, as Queensland's intense UV can affect the healing process. Most clients return to normal activities immediately, though swimming and intense exercise should be avoided for 48 hours.
